\[
\begin{tikzpicture}
\draw[->] (0,0) -- (6,0);
\foreach \x in {0,1,...,5} \draw (\x,0.1) -- (\x,-0.1);
\node[below] at (0,0) {40};
\node[below] at (5,0) {50};
\node[above] at (2.5,0) {?};
\end{tikzpicture}
\]

1. Ecart total : $50 - 40 = 10$.
2. Nombre de pas : 5.
3. Valeur d'un pas : $10 \div 5 = 2$.
